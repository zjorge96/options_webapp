Stock Options by Zachary Jorgensen

My name is Zachary Adam Jorgensen. I was born and raised here in Chico. I started attending Chico State in the Spring of 2020 and will be completing my degree Fall 2022. I love to play video games and code in my free time. My favorite food is sushi.

The thought for creating this app began after the market crash of 2020. I found that I could make a small weekly amount from just the price of an option contract. I decided to write a script to find the best options within the S&P 500.
